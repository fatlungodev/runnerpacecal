How to Use Track Pacing

1. SELECT MODE

Distance Base vs Lap Base

Choose Distance Base for standard race distances (800m, 5k, etc.). Use Lap Base to calculate pacing for specific inner or outer track lanes.

2. DEFINE GOAL

By Pacing vs By Target Time

Toggle By Pacing to see splits for a specific speed (e.g. 3:45/km). Switch to By Target Time to find the pace needed for a total clock time.

3. MANAGE DATA

Save & Copy

Tap the save Save Button to log your splits in History. Use the content_copy Copy Button to share plain-text splits with others.

Privacy Policy

Your data belongs to you. Track Pacing is designed with privacy at its core.

  • No Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.
  • Offline First: All your calculations and history are stored locally on your device.
  • No Tracking: We don't use any trackers or third-party analytics.
